About Our Green Initiative

Our business is dedicated to going green. We pride ourselves in recycling and using cleaning agents that are safe for the earth. You can even use them on your pets!

We want to contribute to the betterment of climate change and encourage others to do so.

To do this, we offer 50% all washes for hybrid and electric cars! Bring them on by, we will be sure to accomodate you.

20% of all proceeds will go the the ‘GO GREEN’ initiative fund founded by the Car Wash Association of this state.
